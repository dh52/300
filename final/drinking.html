Drinking Water

Analysis of organic and inorganic drinking water analytes required by the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA).

TEST METHOD
Organics:
1,2-Dibromoethane (EDB), 1,2-Dibromo-3-Chloropropane (DBCP) EPA 504.1
Chlorinated Pesticides and/or Polychlorinated Biphenyls (PCBs) EPA 508
Chlorinated Herbicides EPA 515.4
Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) EPA 524.2
Synthetic Organic Compounds (SOCs) EPA 525.2
Organophosphorus Pesticides EPA 525.2
Carbamate Pesticides EPA 531.1
Glyphosate EPA 547
Endothall EPA 548.1
Diquat EPA 549.2
Inorganics:
11 Inorganic Rule Metals (Sb, As, Ba, Be, Cd, Cr, Hg, Ni, Se, Na, Tl) EPA 200.8 / 245.1
Fluoride EPA 300.0
Total Cyanide SM 4500-CN E
Disinfection Byproducts:
Total Trihalomethanes (TTHMs) EPA 524.2
Haloacetic Acids (HAAS) EPA 552.2
Alkalinity SM 2320 B
Chlorite EPA 300.1
Bromate EPA 300.1
Dissolved Organic Carbon (DOC) SM 5310 C
Magnesium Hardness EPA 200.8 / SM 2340 B
Specific Ultra Violet (UV)-Absorbance - SUVA (includes DOC and UV 254) SM 5310 C / 5910 B
Total Organic Carbon (TOC) SM 5310 C
UV 254 SM 5910 B
Miscellaneous:
Anions: Br, Cl, F, SO4, NO2, NO3, PO4 EPA 300.0
Lead and Copper EPA 200.8
Langelier Index (Alk, Ca, pH, TDS) /Corrosivity SM 2330 B
