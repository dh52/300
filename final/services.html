Services

Services Overview

Our standard service includes organic analyses using a variety of GC, GC/MS test methods and inorganic analyses using a variety of ICP and Trace ICP, ICP/MS methods. Fremont Analytical also provides services for a full range of wet chemistry services for most general chemistry parameters.

Fremont Analytical performs analyses under various regulatory programs using both published and laboratory developed / validated test methods. In support of these activities, Fremont Analytical is certified by The Washington State Department of Ecology and participates in several national programs.
